ABOUT ME

Android - Mobile Developer

I’m Ali Erçin Akkaya, a Computer Engineering graduate from İzmir Bakırçay University. I have over two years of experience developing Android applications, specializing in Java and Kotlin. I work with modern tools such as Jetpack Compose, MVVM architecture, Retrofit, Hilt, and Firebase to create user-friendly and high-performance applications.

I am also focusing on mastering clean architecture and modular design to build scalable, maintainable, and efficient codebases. I thrive in team environments, enjoy collaborating toward shared goals, and excel at quickly learning and implementing new technologies. My portfolio includes published apps on the Google Play Store and projects where I’ve taken on leadership roles. My mission is to deliver innovative and reliable solutions that make users' lives easier.

I have in-depth experience in developing cross-platform applications with Flutter. I have effectively used Flutter to create high-performance and user-friendly applications for both Android and iOS platforms. By successfully applying modern Flutter techniques such as BLoC, Cubit, and State Management, I have optimized in-app data management and user interaction. Additionally, I have developed flexible and sustainable solutions tailored to users' needs through API integration and customized UI/UX designs. In my Flutter projects, I follow best practices to ensure a consistent and efficient user experience across both platforms.

Coffee App

Google Play link: From Here

"Coffee App" is a platform where users can access all the information they need about a café in an easy-to-use format. Through the app, users can browse the café's menu, view existing branches, and check out the latest posts on the homepage. The system, which allows users to collect points with every coffee purchase and redeem those points for free coffee, makes the user experience more enjoyable. Coffee App provides a modern solution to the needs of coffee lovers, elevating their coffee experience to the next level every day.
